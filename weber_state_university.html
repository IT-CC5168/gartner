Client Success Story

Expanding education through digital migration

Weber State University began a cloud migration journey, aiming to digitize the campus experience and increase satisfaction scores.

They needed to prioritize $60 million in federal funding in order to realize its diginal initiatives, encompassing security and optimizing Oracle-managed services.

We refocused the company on value, guiding the leaders to create a well-developed, cost-effective security-focused and timely cloud migration roadmap.

After an initial tech stack review, Gartner experts guided an engagement strategy with the IT leaders.

What we did:
  • Current technology insight
  • Best-in-class toolkits
  • Advised on contract reviews, where significant longterm cost savings were identified by prioritizing security first in the roadmap to avoid a hasty cloud migration
  • Strategic support utilizing its Gartner Executive Programs subscription
  • Assisted with the evaluation, planning and supporting of the execution of Weber State's move toward digital transformation
